FISAT

Federal Institute of Science And Technology

The FISAT is a public, deemed, research college for higher education and research in science, engineering, design, and management.
It is located in the southern Indian city of Bangalore, Karnataka.
The institute was established in 1909 with active support from Jamsetji Tata and thus is also locally known as the "Tata Institute".

Courses We Offer...

Whether you're a beginner wondering where to start, or an experienced writer looking to extend your craft, we have a part-time, flexible course for you.

Part-Time Award Programmes

Part-time creative writing award-bearing courses for those looking to gain an Oxford qualification.

Under Graduate Programmes

Our part-time undergraduate programmes are delivered in two formats:
you can choose to study mostly online (with one short module taken in Oxford) or you can opt for face-to-face teaching.

Post Graduatee Programmes

A two-year, part-time master's programme offering a clustered learning format of five residences, two guided retreats and one placement over two years.
